
Q1: What is USDC?
A1: USDC is a digital currency, developed by Circle, which is fully collateralized by US Dollars.

Q2: What is USDC-SPL?
A2: USDC-SPL is the integration of USDC with the Solana blockchain, providing a fast, secure, and reliable way to store, send, and receive USDC.

Q3: What are the benefits of using USDC-SPL?
A3: USDC-SPL provides users with a fast, secure, and reliable way to store and transact USDC, with low fees and high scalability.
