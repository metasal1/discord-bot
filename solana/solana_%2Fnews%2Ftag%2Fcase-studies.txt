Q1. What is Dialect?
A1. Dialect is a decentralized social messaging platform that uses compressed NFTs to onboard thousands of users for free.

Q2. What is Crossmint?
A2. Crossmint is an NFT tooling and API company that is using state compression to drop NFTs at a massive scale.

Q3. What is Solana Pay?
A3. Solana Pay is a payment stack that enables smart contract payments. It is used by ASICS to create a decentralized loyalty program, and by Credix to expand access to credit in Brazil. It is also used by Autonomous to create a smart contract payment stack.
