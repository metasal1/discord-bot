Q1: What is Solana?
A1: Solana is a high-throughput, low-fee, low-latency blockchain designed for developers to build applications on.

Q2: What do Solana's features enable?
A2: Solana's features enable fast, seamless, and easy DeFi applications, capital efficiency, and smart contract development.

Q3: What is the Anchor framework?
A3: Anchor is a toolchain that simplifies smart contract development. It includes Solidity, web3.js, ethers.js, Truffle, and ganache all in one.

Q4: What is the benefit of Solana's single global state?
A4: Solana's single global state enables developers to build applications without dealing with capital inefficient Layer 2 systems or sharded chains, and it scales alongside Moore's Law.
